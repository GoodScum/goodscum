### [Unique Animation, Repeated Story](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt36148135/reviews/?featured=rw11180991&ref_=tt_ururv_c_3_hd)

When a plant-like alien suddenly appears, the human race's existence changes. Two years later, they have a new routine until something changes for just two people. Thrust into an endless timeloop, they must work together to figure out what is happening and how to get to tomorrow.  
  
This alien-invasion anime has a story very similar to the Hollywood movie Edge of Tomorrow. The story is bare-bones, offering little backstory and focusing primarily on the present situation. The animation is unique and vibrant, with some well-done action sequences. Despite being only one hour and twenty-six minutes, the movie feels long. It might be worth a stream for its unique animation style.[jaxvanderson](https://www.imdb.com/user/ur160329009/?ref_=tt_ururv_c_4_uname)
