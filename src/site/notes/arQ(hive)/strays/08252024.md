Slide me behind your ear; smoke me later
On, when you need to get some air. Blame me For the happy in your cough. I plead guilty, let me spread across you, slow, a smile you didn't know was coming til I did. Drag me 
From here to wherever you are. Let me linger 
when I'm close enough to love you
