clear water, and muddy, 
will
rise up in you.

struggle to resist,
but unless you subside;
succumb to your feet growing wet,
(a hard task,) 
you will forestall your rising.

fall. make 
many bitter mistakes, and 
suffer therefrom.
drink, but drink
with 
love.

have that passionate awakening to love
and life, without knowing 
how.
