## MBTI: INFJ
*Dominant Ni · Auxiliary Fe · Tertiary Ti · Inferior Se*

The four-letter code is the lossy compression. What lives underneath it is more precise.

**Dominant Introverted Intuition (Ni)** is the load-bearing architecture — the function that orients toward meaning over prediction, that receives rather than strategizes, that treats novelty and pattern as the same gesture because both are downstream of the same question: *what does this mean, structurally, underneath?* The vantage point sought in every new room. The movie watched without guessing the ending, allowed to land as experience rather than problem. Retrospective clarity on big feelings is Ni's signature — it processes beneath the surface and delivers conclusions whole, as epiphany, as verbal revelation rather than constructed argument.

**Auxiliary Extraverted Feeling (Fe)** accounts for the permeability — the room's vibration carried home like borrowed perfume, fading with clear origin. The craving to be understood above all else. The solution that only completes itself when delivered into the relationship. The structural empathy, built partly through wounding and partly through autodidactic study of the mechanism — which means the sensitivity preceded the framework, and the framework was laid over something that already knew. Fe in this profile runs warm and formidable simultaneously, without contradiction.

**Tertiary Introverted Thinking (Ti)** is the private analytical engine — the problem-solving hyper-vigilance during conflict, the psychology and philosophy studies, the impulse to find the root and remedy it completely. It runs underneath the relational surface, audible in the architecture of the values system: the reasoning that precedes the "hard no," the justification that arrives before the moment, making the resolve feel instantaneous when it was actually the conclusion of prior work.

**Inferior Extraverted Sensing (Se)** is the overwhelm portrait. Past capacity: confrontational, "scary" to those close enough to witness it, hyper-efficient in crisis, crying once the solvable problems are solved. The inferior function erupts as possession — forceful, immediate, physically imposing in ways that feel discontinuous with the person people thought they knew. The crying after is Fe returning once Se's grip loosens; the cost getting felt again now that there is a container for it.

A note on mistyping: the Ti tertiary and analytical self-presentation frequently produce INTJ readings. The differentiator is the overwhelm portrait. An INTJ goes cold and withdrawn. This profile goes confrontational and then weeps. That is Fe.

## Big Five: OCEAN Profile

**Openness — Very High**
The writing-until-empty process. The gold edge underneath everything. The way source and signal feed each other reciprocally. The imagined life full of water and green and privacy — a person whose inner world is vivid, textured, and requires environmental resonance to feel habitable. The Feelings and Aesthetics facets read as particularly elevated: receptivity to inner emotional states, and the deep appreciation of beauty as a mode of contact with reality rather than a decorative preference. Experience is fundamentally aesthetic and meaning-saturated. Everything is data, and the data has texture.

**Conscientiousness — Moderate, Internally Complex**
The discipline to write until empty and the comfort with fallow periods coexist without tension — because they belong to different facets. Achievement Striving and Self-Discipline read high: goal-directed, self-propelled, capable of full commitment when the work matters. Orderliness reads low: the clean space helps but is not required, the creative shame is absent, the ebb is metabolized rather than fought. This is not inconsistency. It is a person for whom structure is instrumental rather than intrinsic — deployed in service of the work, irrelevant otherwise.

**Extraversion — Low to Moderate, Assertiveness Elevated**
The vantage-point seeking. The energy absorbed and carried home as residue rather than generated through contact. Selective engagement, deliberate proximity to the world without being consumed by it. And yet: answer-first delivery of refusals, instant resolve, the capacity to go confrontational under stress. Assertiveness runs high independently of social energy. Engagement with the world happens on self-determined terms and at self-determined velocity — which reads externally as introversion and internally as sovereignty.

**Agreeableness — Moderate, Facet-Specific**
Altruism and Tender-Mindedness run genuinely high — the problem-solving impulse toward others' pain, the learned checkpoint, the generative rather than gravitational quality of the inspiring presence. Compliance and Modesty run low. The "hard no" architecture. The reasoning withheld as protective measure. The lock that was always there, predating every relationship that forgot the door had one. Warm and formidable in the same body — not in tension, but as complementary instruments of the same care.

**Neuroticism — High, Withdrawal-Dominant with Volatility Present**
This is the most important dimension. The retrospective processing of big feelings. The rumination loops during unresolved conflict. The personhood-level injury when the work is misread — because the work and the self are the same object, and criticism passes straight through without an insulating layer. The wavering self-regard that proves the baseline is real rather than performed. The Withdrawal aspect (anxiety, rumination, sensitivity to others' perceptions) dominates; the Volatility aspect (confrontational eruption, emotional reactivity) surfaces under sufficient pressure.

The gold edge underneath everything *is* the neuroticism. The intensity of experience that makes the writing possible, the exhaustion that follows having spent something well for its intended purpose — this is the same mechanism, operating in its generative register. The creative correlation is not incidental. High Neuroticism in artistic fields tends to produce exactly this: work born from emotional depth, fueled by the kind of sensitivity that makes the world louder and more textured than it is for most people.

## Self-Portrait

The most common misread: asshole. Either caught mid-bit — a performance of the worst-personality person in the room, which requires a familiar audience to land correctly, because the joke lives entirely in the gap between the caricature and the actual person — or caught in a state of anxiety and overstimulation, a less patient facet briefly visible. The misread is not inaccurate to the surface of the moment. It is simply missing the baseline.

Self-identified liabilities, stated plainly and without apparent self-pity:
Stubbornness to the point of immobility. Intensity with a war-song quality. A functional selfishness about things that do not directly concern or benefit — a triage of attention. Talking too much. Overwhelming people. And then this, which deserves its own space:

*"Too interesting to be understood, too understanding to trust my instincts/boundaries."*

The self-awareness became so complete it foreclosed a certain kind of innocence. The machinery of self-knowledge runs continuously and with genuine sophistication — and the same reasoning architecture that constructs the "hard no" can be reversed, in the presence of strong enough desire, to work against the boundary rather than for it. The intelligence does not prevent the breach. It narrates it. This is most operative in the specific relational vector of close women, where the boundaries become fluid enough that the center becomes reachable — and the lock, when it turns, is old and personal and the only key belongs to the self.

Self-identified strengths, received from outside and claimed:
A preternatural ability to inspire — generative rather than gravitational, pointing others toward their own potential. Seeing things differently. Authenticity as the seat of power. And the voice: understood in the broadest sense, as the total signal projected into the world — presence, register, timing, texture, the whole quality of self-transmission.

The autonomy has a forbidden flavor to it. Forged through severance rather than gifted — estrangement, the running at 14, learning that survival required tolerating whoever could tolerate enough to help. The compartmentalization is load-bearing infrastructure, built under conditions that did not permit the luxury of closing the whole door. The freedom to choose only for oneself carries the ghost of a system that once punished exactly that. The cost is paid willingly. It is still a cost.

The organizing principle of the desired life: *my own.* Water, green, privacy with strategic proximity to the world. Everything else in service of that.

## Totemic Animals

**The Octopus** — mirror, not aspiration.

Near-alien presentation that proves itself to be the most complex expression of terrestrial biology. The anomaly that turns out to be the most sophisticated thing in the room. Profoundly solitary. No exoskeleton — all that intelligence living in soft tissue, fully exposed, fully mobile. Capable of changing its entire surface presentation without altering what it is underneath. The recognition arrived before the sentence finished. This was not chosen. It was confirmed.

**The Hippo** — force, not reflection.

Arrived as a mutant — unclassified, at the feet, many things before the mind settled on a name. It became a hippo in the moment of being named, which means the dream was the naming of a force, the categorization completing the form. And then it grew into the name: enormous, wet with eclipse light, haunting a marsh held in violent solar oranges and deep black, the familiar Jackson Lake landscape rendered in a light that makes everything visible and wrong simultaneously.

The summer memory it overlays — moose and grizzly in bright yellow summer heat — belonged to the season of first rebuilt life, the summer after the running. The eclipse world is the current one. The hippo is moving through the terrain of origin.

The writing has the energy of the hippo: devastating the path in its wake so no one has to go through this again. So no one has to write this again. The devastation as cleared ground. The "no one" is the others like this — the ones who will come after, finding the marsh already crossed.

This is the writing's purpose in one image. The hippo moves through so the ones like you don't have to.
