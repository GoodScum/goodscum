### moldavite — 2016-11 `#12128933`

2013 - Trinidad, Colorado

:::::

we made a key to other worlds out of odds and ends around the farm.

a wooden skewer. a chicken feather. some old wire. a bubble wand. among other things.

our packs, we filled with sweet chilled coconut chai, awkward sweet potato hotcakes, and divination tools. 

among other things.

we ventured forth with three big friendly dogs at our sides, and two sassy goats at our heels. 

our pockets filled rapidly with sticky pine resin as we walked our talk and hunted for raw rose quartz at the top of the ridge, wondering:

"at what point are you in the sky and not just off the ground?"

we rattled our key to other dimensions.

:::::

### moldavite — 2016-11 `#12128982`

the night that i learned how to operate a kerosene lamp, we chose to sleep in the cabin up the hill, instead of in our tipis. 

we were told: in that cabin, years before, a sleep-walking man threw himself from the upstairs window. 

his anxiety seemed to linger; his fear tried to pretend it was my own. 

with all of the authority of two young witches, soft earth perpetually lodged beneath our nails, we cleansed that space.

replaced tension with sweet dreams, security, and remedial sign language lessons. 

we wove peace into the cobblestones - more for future inhabitants than ourselves. 

and together, surrounded by sequoias, we slept.

:::::
