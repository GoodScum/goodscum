this is a true story

i went on a hitchhiking trip down the coast of california for a couple of months, w/ minimal money. right before i left someone found a kitten and didnt know what to do with him, so i decided to bring him with me. this was a naive mistake i learned later, but i cared for him v well despite being regularly unable to take of myself. his name was Lucifer.

On dec 23, my personal holiday to celebrate the day i ran away from my family, i ate some lsd, which is the tradition. the rest of the story told all occurred at the peak of my trip:

Lucifer was very friendly and precocious for his size and lunged for a toy that belonged to a dog one of my travel buddies at the time had. though dog and kitty were normally friends, and got along well, dog attacked on impulse due to some past abuse and snapped at Lucifer's head. Not exactly sure what the damage was, as I had to take him to the vet under the pretense that I had found him stray and absolve any ownership of him that I had in order to get him admitted w/out paying the outrageously expensive check in and examination fees, as i was v literally flat broke at the time.

One of the people i was traveling with had a vet tech friend who they called and let me talk to. it seemed like his skull had cracked, and that he was probably hemorrhaging. The vet tech friend was not optimistic about his survival. His right eye bulged out of his face and became cloudy, and a steady stream of blood came from his nose and mouth all night (no vet centers were open til morning b/c of the holidays. i sat w/ him in the bus i was traveling in all night and cried and cried).

To this day I don't know what happened to him after I left him at the vet. This all happened in fucking Las Vegas, to top it all off.

The end.
