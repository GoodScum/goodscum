<p style="font-size: 0.8rem; color: #606068; margin-top: 0.4em;">
The first sans-serif design. Chromatic aberration as design language — link text carries a subtle RGB displacement (<span style="text-shadow: -0.5px 0 rgba(224,64,80,0.4), 0.5px 0 rgba(80,128,224,0.4); color: #a0a0aa;">like this</span>) that collapses to clean white on hover. Numbered vector list instead of categorized groups — no taxonomy imposed, just an ordered basis. Instrument Serif appears only in headings and blockquotes (the collapsed, resolved state). The loneliest design; also the most technically expressive.

## All six at a glance

| | **Nacre** | **Eigenvector** | **Carrier Freq** | **Marsh Crossing** | **Mirror-Hive** | **Hilbert** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Mode** | dark | dark | dark | **light** | dark | dark |
| **Feel** | contemplative | austere | warm analog | open, breathing | architectural | phase-shifted |
| **Font system** | thin serif + mono | mono + serif-quotes | warm serif + mono | book serif + mono | optical serif + mono | **sans-serif** + serif-collapse |
| **Color logic** | spectral layers | one surviving red | amber gradient | earth greens on paper | honey + glass | RGB chromatic split |
| **Nav structure** | tile grid | flat list | 3-col dial | **2-col unordered** | **hex cell grid** | numbered vectors |
| **Organizing** | by theme | by axis | by function | **none** | by verb | by index |
| **Unique element** | iridescent `<hr>` | `→` prefix on h1 | `~ ~ ~` waveform | daylight itself | clip-path hexagons | text-shadow displacement |
| **Random btn** | enter the shell | collapse a wave function | tune to something | or wander | open a cell | measure something |
| **Posture** | draws inward | trusts the writing | invites browsing | lets you walk | holds you | **performs** the problem |
