## At a glance

| | **Nacre** | **Eigenvector** | **Carrier Frequency** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Feel** | Contemplative, oceanic | Austere, mathematical | Warm, analog |
| **Entry** | Centered, vertical | Left-aligned, sparse | Top-down, columnar |
| **Palette** | Deep blue-black + shell spectrum | Pure black + one red | Warm black + amber/copper |
| **Body font** | Cormorant Garamond (thin serif) | JetBrains Mono (monospace) | Literata (warm serif) |
| **UI font** | IBM Plex Mono | JetBrains Mono (same) | Space Mono |
| **Headings** | Ultra-light, color-per-level | Lowercase, arrow-prefixed | Gold filament, copper labels |
| **Dividers** | Iridescent gradient line | Grey line + red center dot | Ember line + ~~~ waveform |
| **Navigation** | Tile grid (aragonite tablets) | Flat list with em-dashes | Three-column dial |
| **Random btn** | "enter the shell" | "collapse a wave function" | "tune to something" |
| **Best for** | The reader who lingers | The reader who trusts the work | The reader who browses |
