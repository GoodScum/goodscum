## Hilbert family — all four

| | **Hilbert** (original) | **Observer** | **Entanglement** | **Decoherence** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Core idea** | superposition → collapse on hover | homepage IS the randomizer | two random pieces as entangled pair | two populations, one boundary |
| **Navigation** | numbered vector list | single encounter + ghost cloud | split-screen pair + ghost cloud | phase diagram: measured vs. uncollapsed |
| **Randomizer role** | option alongside list | **the entire homepage** | **the entire homepage** (x2) | restricted to uncollapsed pool |
| **Chromatic effect** | RGB split on titles | RGB split on the encounter | red/blue split by particle | RGB split on uncollapsed only |
| **Resolution** | hover collapses text-shadow | clicking navigates = measurement | pairing generates new meaning | reading migrates pieces across boundary |
| **Unique element** | layered superposition title | probability cloud (near-invisible) | interference seam between particles | phase-boundary dots |
| **Posture** | performs the problem | refuses to let you browse | says meaning lives in juxtaposition | says the garden changes as you use it |
| **Random btn** | measure something | observe again | entangle again | collapse something new |
