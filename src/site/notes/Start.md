This is a garden grown from a boneyard.

I will string threads between threads until the web is strong enough to catch the fluttering passersby and hold them still for awhile

The writing here spans about a decade. Some of it was made for online communities; 
some was made in private and never shown to anyone. A garden makes no distinction 
between the two.
